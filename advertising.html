Last updated: 20/10/2025

Some offers and recommendations on PrizePaths are “affiliate” or “sponsored” links. This means:

  • If you click a link and take an action (such as signing up, starting a free trial, or purchasing), we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

  • All items and offers remain subject to availability and any terms set by the partner.

Our Editorial Approach

We aim to feature offers that are relevant and valuable to our users. Compensation may influence the order in which offers appear, but it does not require you to purchase or sign up for anything. You are always free to choose which offers, if any, are right for you.
