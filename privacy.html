PrizePaths — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how PrizePaths Pty Ltd (“PrizePaths,” “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website, surveys, or services (collectively, the “Website” or “Service”).

By accessing or using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Website.

1. Who Controls Your Information

Any information collected through PrizePaths is controlled by:

PrizePaths Pty Ltd

Email: support@prizepaths.com

Website: www.prizepaths.com

Registered in: Australia (ABN pending registration)

For users located in the United States, PrizePaths operates in compliance with applicable U.S. privacy laws and industry standards, and data may be stored and processed in the United States or Australia.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information when you use our Website, participate in surveys, claim giveaways, or interact with our promotional partners.

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • Responses to survey questions and giveaway forms.
  • Reviews, testimonials, or public comments you submit.
  • Any messages or communications you send to us.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Pages visited, clicks, and time spent on the Website.
  • Referral URLs and geographic location (approximate).
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (see Section 6 below).

c. Information from Partners

PrizePaths may receive information from affiliate networks, brands, or third-party partners to help fulfill offers or validate entries.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Administer giveaways and promotions (subject to availability).
  • Communicate with you regarding prizes, updates, or partner offers.
  • Send newsletters, promotional content, and partner opportunities.
  • Personalize your experience and improve the Website.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent use.

Your participation in surveys or giveaways constitutes consent for PrizePaths to contact you about relevant offers, unless you unsubscribe.

4. Sharing Your Information

PrizePaths may share your information in the following cases:

Recipient Purpose
Partner Brands and Affiliates To fulfill sample or prize delivery, validate entries, or offer related promotions.
Service Providers To manage email delivery, hosting, analytics, or marketing automation systems.
Legal or Regulatory Authorities When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
Successor Entities In the event of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets.

We do not sell your personal data for monetary gain. However, certain data sharing may be considered a “sale” under U.S. law (e.g., for targeted advertising). You may opt out at any time (see Section 9).

5. Subject to Availability

All giveaways, free products, and samples offered through PrizePaths are subject to availability.

  • We cannot guarantee that every user will receive a product.
  • Selection for free items is determined at our discretion based on eligibility, stock, and partner participation.
  • Offers may change or end without notice.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, tracking pixels, and similar tools to:

  • Authenticate users and manage sessions.
  • Analyse site performance and improve functionality.
  • Deliver personalized promotions or retargeting ads.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features.

Third-Party Tracking: We may allow advertising or analytics partners (e.g., Google Analytics) to collect information about your activity to help us serve relevant content.

7. How We Protect Your Information

PrizePaths uses industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and secure hosting.

However, no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure. You acknowledge that you provide information at your own risk.

We retain data only as long as necessary for operational, legal, or legitimate business purposes.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location (Australia, California, or elsewhere), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent from promotional communications or targeted advertising.
  • Complaint: Lodge a privacy complaint with the OAIC (Australia) or the FTC (U.S.).

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@prizepaths.com. We may require identity verification before processing requests.

9. Marketing and Communication Preferences

By submitting your information, you consent to receive marketing communications from PrizePaths and our affiliates.

You can unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Clicking “unsubscribe” in our emails, or
  • Emailing support@prizepaths.com.

Even if you unsubscribe, we may still contact you for non-promotional reasons (e.g., giveaway results, account updates).

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside Australia or the United States, your personal data may be transferred to and stored in either country. By using our Website, you consent to this transfer and acknowledge that data protection laws in these countries may differ from your own jurisdiction.

11. Children’s Privacy

PrizePaths does not knowingly collect or solicit information from anyone under 18 years of age. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor without verified parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

12. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our Website may contain links to external offers, surveys, or partner promotions. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites. You should review the privacy policy of each external site before submitting information.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Significant changes will be communicated by email or prominent notice on our Website.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, please contact us at:

PrizePaths Pty Ltd

Email: support@prizepaths.com

Website: www.prizepaths.com

If you are in Australia, you may also contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au. U.S. residents may contact the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) for privacy complaints.

15. Compliance Summary

This Privacy Policy has been structured to align with:

  • Australia: Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • USA: CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), FTC Act, and CAN-SPAM Act
